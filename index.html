About Me

I am currently a Research Scholar at the Department of Electrical Engineering, IIT Madras, working under the supervision of Prof. Kaushik Mitra and Dr. Mansi Sharma. I hold an M.Tech in VLSI and Embedded Systems Design from NIT Manipur (2020) and a B.E. in Electronics and Communication from B.M.S. College of Engineering, Bengaluru (2017).

Research Interests

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, generative AI, and image processing. My work focuses on inferring the physical world (shape, motion, color, light) from images, often using radiance fields. Some papers are highlighted.

Selected Works

CAT4D: Create Anything in 4D with Multi-View Video Diffusion Models

CVPR, 2025 (Oral)

An approach for turning a video into a 4D radiance field that can be rendered in real-time. When combined with a text-to-video model, this enables text-to-4D.
